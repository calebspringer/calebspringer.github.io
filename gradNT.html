Number Theory

This page is an archive of talks which were given by grad students in the 2020-2021 school year at Penn State.

Since we haven't been able to see each other in person recently, the graduate students studying algebra, number theory and related topics at Penn State are holding regular meetings via Zoom. Each week, one student volunteers to discuss some aspect of their research.

We meet at 11 AM on Tuesdays, unless something else comes up. The Zoom link is only accessible via authenticated Penn State accounts.
